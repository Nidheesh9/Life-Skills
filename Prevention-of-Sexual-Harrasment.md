## Question 2: What would you do if you face or witness such behaviour?

If I face harassment or see it happening to someone else, I would:

- Take even small incidents seriously, as they can grow into bigger problems.
- Keep notes of what happened, including date, time, place, and people involved.
- Report the issue to the Internal Complaints Committee (ICC), HR, or a manager as soon as possible.
- Support the affected person and encourage them to speak up.
- Stay calm and clear, and tell the person that their behaviour is not acceptable.
- Follow company policies and trust the process to handle the issue fairly.
