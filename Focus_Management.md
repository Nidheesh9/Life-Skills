## Question 4

**What are the dangers of social media, in brief?**

**Answer**

- Professional Harm:
  Social media breaks your focus and makes it harder to do deep, important work, which can hurt your success.

- Psychological Harm:
  It increases loneliness, comparison, and anxiety because your brain gets overloaded with constant posts and notifications.
