## Question 2

**How can you use B = MAP to make making new habits easier? What are M, A and P.**

**Answer**

I can use these things to make new habits easier:
* Shrink the habit.
* Identify an action prompt.
* Shine after doing my new habit.
* Don't rely on motivation.

M, A, and P are:

* Motivation – Don’t rely on motivation.
* Ability – How easy the habit is to do.
* Prompt – A reminder to do a new habit.
